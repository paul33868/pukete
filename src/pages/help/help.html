Help

Hello, welcome to Pukete

The app that will help you to manage money between you and your friends.

Pressing the "Start" button will take you to the main page in which you can do the maths.

The icon adds a new person and allows you to enter the name and the expenses. That person will be added and counted on the "All" tag. Also if you wish, you can add a title for the event.

The "tags" are the types of expenses that person is counted on. If you like to remove any tag, click on the person's name or any tag.

The available tags are:

All The person is included on food, drinks and others.

Nothing The person has paid but it didn't consume, so it'll get all the money back.

Drinks Food Others

After you finished adding or editing everyone, click on the calculator icon to see how much each one has to give or get from the pot.

The lists are saved once you do the maths or you can press the icon.

At the end, you can share anywhre you want.

Anytime, you can press the icon to display all the options.
